**CHAPTER 8**

*SKATEPARK ETIQUETTE, OR: HOW NOT TO BE THAT PERSON*

At some point, you're going to go to a skatepark. This is exciting. This is also nerve-wracking, because skateparks have their own unwritten rules and it's possible to accidentally be very annoying without knowing it.

So let's write them down.

*[The critters at a little skatepark. Baby Skate Bunny skating a small ramp. Squirrel waiting patiently at the top for her turn. Duck rolling along the flat. Jay perched on a bench watching. Turtle slowly cruising in the background. Everyone taking turns, good vibes. A tiny pink cloud.]*
